### About OE_Sprints

**OE_Sprints** is a short-term funding program supporting innovative, needs-based developments in the OER ecosystem. Projects are implemented in an agile and collaborative manner to quickly close infrastructural gaps and strengthen open educational practices.

Its main goals include:

* Supporting open and interoperable infrastructures
* Promoting inclusive and accessible learning solutions
* Encouraging collaboration and community-driven innovation
* Reducing technical and organizational barriers

### What LiaEx Will Deliver

LiaEx will enhance the existing LiaScript-Exporter into a versatile tool that allows educational content created with LiaScript to be used across different **Learning Management Systems (LMS)** and **open educational formats**.

A key goal is to enable seamless export and integration of LiaScript courses into systems such as **Moodle**, **ILIAS**, and **OpenOLAT**, as well as conversion into **H5P** packages.
